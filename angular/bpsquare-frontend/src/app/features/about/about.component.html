Our Mission

Helping Small Businesses Turn Manual Work Into Useful Digital Systems

BPSquare LLC is in its first year of service, founded on a straightforward belief: service-based small businesses deserve access to thoughtful full-stack engineering, not watered-down tools or oversized consulting that does not fit their reality.

The early focus is narrow on purpose: custom web apps, internal tools, workflow automation, small business operations modernization, WordPress + Angular integrations, and custom WordPress theme development.

BPSquare is currently working with early potential clients, including volunteer projects, to build a body of real work. The promise is honest: focused attention, practical scope, production-minded engineering, and clear communication from the person doing the work.
